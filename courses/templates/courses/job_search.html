Job Search Tips

Effective Job Search Strategies

  • Network: Reach out to alumni, professors, and professionals on platforms like LinkedIn.
  • Use Job Boards: Check sites like JobFinder, Indeed, and LinkedIn for opportunities.
  • Research Companies: Target companies that align with your values and career goals.
  • Apply Early: Submit applications as soon as possible to increase your chances.
  • Follow Up: Send a polite follow-up email after applying to show interest.
