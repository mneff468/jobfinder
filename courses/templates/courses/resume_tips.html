Resume Tips

Build a Winning Resume

  • Tailor Your Resume: Customize your resume for each job by highlighting relevant skills and experiences.
  • Keep It Concise: Aim for a one-page resume unless you have extensive experience.
  • Use Action Verbs: Start bullet points with strong verbs like "developed," "led," or "analyzed."
  • Quantify Achievements: Include numbers to show impact (e.g., "Increased sales by 20%").
  • Proofread: Double-check for typos and grammatical errors to make a professional impression.
